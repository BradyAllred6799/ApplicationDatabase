### Meeting Summary: CCS Planning Session 1 — Gifts & Endowments

**Purpose:** Align on demos for Gifts & Endowments, define audiences, add level-setting content (current vs. future; "source of fund" to "gift" mapping), and set pacing and deliverables for the session.

**Decisions and Demo Scope**

- **Level-setting and terminology slides** *(before demos)*
  - Current vs. future overview; "when we say gift, we mean…"; mapping from current source of fund to Workday gift; where departments tap in (external form initiation; planned "gift set up" notification).
  - *Audience:* Foundation core office as primary; departmental SMEs (informational) to understand touchpoints.

- **Demo 1: Create/Edit Gift** *(core Workday BP)*
  - Create a gift from a clean example; then edit a value to show update path; note the short, one-step BP and that much intake occurs via external form; optionally show quick maintenance of gift attribute values (e.g., adding a new purpose code) if helpful.

- **Demo 2: Gift hierarchy placement and Gift Manager assignment**
  - Place the newly created gift into the hierarchy; show resulting auto-assignment of Gift Manager and access implications; note that hierarchy placement can also be done via EIB (bulk), but the UI path will be shown.

- **Demo 3: Reporting touchpoints**
  - Show review report of gifts created/edited over a period (post-BP quality check), the gift hierarchy report, and the Gift Listing (FDM reference) with filters like created/last edited; tie back to current-state concepts where helpful.

**Clarifications**

- Only one formal Workday BP (Create/Edit Gift); other tasks (attribute maintenance, hierarchy placement, reviews) are ancillary but integral to operations.
- Departments will continue to initiate via the external UFFA form; explore adding a "gift set up" notification back to requestors ("more to come").
- Short Q&A after each demo; T&OD team will handle opening slides; James' content begins at slide 14.

**Timing**

- Session will be split into three demos (plus optional EIB), each followed by a brief Q&A; Brady will timebox.
