# Teams Chat Introducing The Alt Text Generator

For those who are struggling with alt text and knowing what to write, I created this copilot agent wherein you can paste or upload an image to it, and it will generate alt text for that image. It does a solid job, usually about as well as I would do.

you can just put the image in and not include any prompt but I always get the best results when I explain the context of the image a bit or paste in some of the surrounding text of whatever I am taking the image from.

You can pin this in your copilot app so it always appears on the right hand side.

For AI enthusiasts, I have had the best results with Gemini, which I also have an agent for, but I know not everyone on the team has a pro account.
