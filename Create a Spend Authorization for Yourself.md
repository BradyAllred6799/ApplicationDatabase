# Creating a Spend Authorization for Yourself

## Overview

This guide walks you through the process of creating a Spend Authorization for yourself in Workday. Use this process when you need authorization for your own anticipated travel expenses before the trip begins.

**Note:** Before completing a Spend Authorization, confirm that you have direct deposit set up to receive reimbursement. Refer to the Getting Started: Payment Enrollment resource for guidance.

## Before You Begin

1. Confirm your travel dates and destinations.

2. Have your cost center, grant, gift, or other funding source information available.

3. Confirm whether the trip involves international travel, split funding, a cash advance, or personal travel days, as these affect how the form is completed.

4. Know whether you plan to bring any UF-owned property on the trip.

5. Gather any required supporting documentation, such as a conference agenda or registration confirmation.

## Navigating to the Form

1. Log in to Workday.

2. Select the search bar on the home page.

3. Type Create Spend Authorization and select that task from the auto-populated results.

![Workday search bar showing 'Create Spend Authorization' and the matching task in the auto-populated dropdown.]

## Step 1: Complete the Header Information

1. The header section captures the essential identifying details of your Spend Authorization.

2. In the Company field, confirm or enter your company. This field may auto-populate based on your profile. Ensure the company matches your profile. For example, a University of Florida employee should show University of Florida, while a Foundation employee should show UFND.

3. In the Start Date and End Date fields, enter the first and last days of your travel period. If the trip involves multiple destinations, the end date should reflect when you return to your origin so that the entire trip is covered under one Spend Authorization.

4. In the Description field, enter a clear and concise title for the Spend Authorization. For example: *Workday Rising Conference – San Francisco*.

5. In the Business Purpose field, select the option from the dropdown that best reflects the purpose of the travel. This field is required and the Spend Authorization cannot be submitted without it. Example values include *Conference/Professional Development/Workshop*.

![Spend Authorization Information section with fields for Company, Start Date, End Date, Description, and Business Purpose.]

1. In the Reimbursement Payment Type field, confirm the value. This field typically auto-populates as **Direct Deposit**.

2. In the Justification field, provide a brief explanation using the five Ws: **who, what, when, where, and why**, describing the benefit of this travel to UF. This field is required even though it does not display a red asterisk.

**Note:** Both the Business Purpose and Justification fields are required in order to submit the Spend Authorization, even though not all required fields display a red asterisk.

![Spend Authorization Details section showing Reimbursement Payment Type as Direct Deposit and a Justification field.]

## Step 2: Add a Spend Authorization Line

1. Each line represents a destination or funding source. You must add at least one line. Add a new line for each of the following situations: each destination in the trip, each separate funding source (split funding), and any cash advance request.

2. Select Add below the header information section.

![Add button with a plus icon, located under the Spend Authorization Lines tab.]

### Expense Item and Amount

1. In the Expense Item field, select the type of travel that applies. Common options include *Travel Out of State*, *Travel In State*, *Travel International*, and *Comp Travel*.

2. In the Total Amount field, enter the total estimated amount you expect to spend for this line. For example, if you expect to spend $1,500 on a domestic trip, enter that amount here.

**Note:** If funding is coming from multiple sources (split funding), create a separate line for each funding source with the appropriate amount and worktags for each source.

1. In the Budget Date field, enter the start date of the trip. This is the date the transaction will post.

2. In the Memo field, describe what you expect to spend the money on and provide estimated amounts for each expense. These amounts should add up to the value in the Total Amount field. For example: Airfare $600, Hotel $500, Conference Registration $250, Per Diem $150.

**Note:** The Memo field is not technically required by the system, but it is required in practice. Approvers rely on the memo to understand how funds will be used.

### Cash Advance (If Applicable)

1. If you require a cash advance, check the Cash Advance Requested box. This is only appropriate in specific circumstances, such as travel to a remote international location where normal payment methods are unavailable.

**Warning: Do not check Cash Advance Requested unless you have confirmed in advance that you are authorized to receive one. Most travelers will not need this option.**

1. If a cash advance is requested, enter the justification in the Cash Advance Justification field in the Item Details section on the right side of the page. Set the total amount for this line to the amount of cash being requested.

![Spend Authorization Line details showing Expense Item, Total Amount, Budget Date, Memo, and the Cash Advance Requested checkbox.]

### Worktags (Funding Sources)

1. In the Worktags subsection, enter the appropriate funding information for this line.

2. If using a **designated** funding source, enter the designated worktag. The Cost Center, Program, and Additional Worktags fields will auto-populate but can be adjusted if needed.

3. If using a **grant** or **gift**, enter the appropriate worktag in the corresponding Grant or Gift field.

4. If funding is split across multiple sources, each source must have its own line with the correct worktags and amount.

![Worktags section displaying fields for Grant, Gift, Designated, Project, Cost Center, Program, and Additional Worktags.]

### Item Details

1. In the Attendees field, list all individuals who will be traveling as part of this Spend Authorization, including any guest travelers. To add a guest, select the dropdown in the Attendees field and choose Guest, then enter the guest's name and, optionally, their title and company.

2. In the Origination field, enter the city or location you are departing from.

3. In the Destination field, enter the city or location you are going to. Both fields are required.

4. If you plan to bring any UF-owned property on the trip, such as a laptop, university-issued device, or lab equipment, check the Bringing UF Property on the Trip box.

**Note:** For international travel, if UF property is being brought along, you will also see instructions to register the property with myAssets. A link to the myAssets portal will be provided on the form.

1. If you will be taking any personal travel during the trip period, enter the personal travel start and end dates in the Personal Travel Start Date and Personal Travel End Date fields. If no personal travel applies, leave these fields blank.

2. If this is an international travel Spend Authorization, complete the Foreign Entities field by listing all international locations you plan to visit.

![Item Details section showing Attendee(s), Origination, Destination, Bringing UF Property on the Trip checkbox, and Personal Travel Dates.]

## Step 3: Add Attachments

1. At the top of the page, select the More dropdown and choose Attachments. This creates the Attachments tab at the bottom of the page.

2. Either drag and drop your file into the attachment area or select Select Files to browse and upload.

3. After the file uploads successfully, confirm it appears under the Attachments tab. Add a comment if needed, then select Upload to include the attachment with the Spend Authorization.

**Note:** Some business purposes require an attachment in order to submit the Spend Authorization. Check with your department designee or refer to UF travel directives to determine whether an attachment is required for the selected business purpose.

![Attachments tab displaying a drop area for files and a 'Select files' button.]

## Step 4: Review and Submit

1. Review all information entered on the Spend Authorization carefully to confirm it is complete and accurate before submitting.

2. Select Submit at the bottom left of the page to submit the Spend Authorization.

![Action buttons at the bottom of the screen showing Submit, Save for Later, and Cancel.]

## After Submitting

Once submitted, the Spend Authorization will route through an approval workflow to your manager. If the Spend Authorization is for international travel, you will also be prompted to complete an international travel attestation at that stage.

**Note:** If you need to leave the form before completing it, select Save to return to it later. Select Cancel only if you need to discard all entries and start over.
