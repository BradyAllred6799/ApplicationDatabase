- **Motion and Animation (WCAG 2.3.3):** Reduce or disable non-essential animations to support users who are sensitive to motion.
- **Consistent Navigation (WCAG 3.2.3):** Keep navigation controls (e.g., Next, Back, Menu) in consistent locations across slides to ensure a predictable experience.
- **Flashing Content (WCAG 2.3.2):** Review courses for any flashing or rapidly animated content exceeding three flashes per second, as this can pose risks for users with photosensitive conditions.

- **Keyboard Accessibility:** Confirm that all interactions are fully operable via keyboard, including logical Tab/Shift+Tab navigation and proper activation of controls using Enter/Space.
